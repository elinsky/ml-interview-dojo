# Bibliography

Complete reference list for all citations used in the ML Interview Dojo notes.

## Books

### Machine Learning & Deep Learning

**chollet2018deep**
- Chollet, François. *Deep Learning with Python*. Manning Publications, 2018.

**goodfellow2016deep**
- Goodfellow, Ian, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville. *Deep Learning*. MIT Press, 2016.
- Available online: http://www.deeplearningbook.org/

**nielsen2015neural**
- Nielsen, Michael A. *Neural Networks and Deep Learning*. Determination Press, 2015.
- Available online: http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/

**geron2017hands**
- Géron, Aurélien. *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn and TensorFlow*. O'Reilly Media, 2017.

**russell2010artificial**

**kuhn2013applied**
- Kuhn, Max, and Kjell Johnson. *Applied Predictive Modeling*. Springer, 2013.

**james2013introduction**

- Available online: https://www.statlearning.com/

**hastie2013elements**
- Hastie, Trevor, Robert Tibshirani, and Jerome Friedman. *The Elements of Statistical Learning*. 2nd ed. Springer, 2013.
- Available online: https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn/

**chatterjee2012regression**
- Chatterjee, Samprit, and Ali S. Hadi. *Regression Analysis by Example*. 5th ed. Wiley, 2012.

**harrell2015regression**
- Harrell, Frank E., Jr. *Regression Modeling Strategies*. 2nd ed. Springer, 2015.

**wasserman2004all**
- Wasserman, Larry. *All of Statistics*. Springer, 2004.

**wilcox2010fundamentals**
- Wilcox, Rand R. *Fundamentals of Modern Statistical Methods*. 2nd ed. Springer, 2010.
