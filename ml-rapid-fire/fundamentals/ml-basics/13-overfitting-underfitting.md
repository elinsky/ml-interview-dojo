# Overfitting vs Underfitting

**Q:** What is overfitting vs underfitting?

**A:**

**Overfitting**:
- Model too **tailored to training data** (learns noise)
- **Generalizes poorly** to unseen data
- Diagnostic: high train accuracy, low test accuracy

**Underfitting**:
- Model **too simple** to capture patterns
- Diagnostic: low accuracy on both train and test
