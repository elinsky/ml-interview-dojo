# How to Reduce Variance

**Q:** How do you reduce variance (fix overfitting)?

**A:**

- **More training data** (single best method)
- **Simpler model** (fewer parameters)
- **Regularization** (L1, L2)
- **Feature selection** / dimensionality reduction
- **Early stopping**
