# R-Squared (Coefficient of Determination)

**Q:** What is R² and how do you interpret it?

**A:**

- R² = SSR / SST = 1 - (SSE / SST)
- **Proportion of variance in Y explained by the model**
- R² = 0: model no better than predicting mean
- R² = 1: perfect predictions
- Also equals [Cor(Y, Ŷ)]²
