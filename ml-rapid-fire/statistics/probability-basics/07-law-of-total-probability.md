# Law of Total Probability

**Q:** What is the law of total probability?

**A:**

- P(A) = Σ P(A|Bᵢ) · P(Bᵢ)
- Bᵢ must partition the sample space
- Useful when A depends on multiple scenarios
- Often used with Bayes' theorem
