# Chain Rule of Probability

**Q:** What is the chain rule of probability?

**A:**

- P(A,B,C) = P(A) · P(B|A) · P(C|A,B)
- Decomposes joint into conditionals
- Order doesn't matter (different decompositions equal)
- Foundation for probabilistic graphical models
