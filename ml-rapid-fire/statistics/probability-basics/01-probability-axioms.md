# Probability Axioms

**Q:** What are the three axioms of probability?

**A:**

- P(A) ≥ 0 for any event A
- P(Ω) = 1 (sample space has probability 1)
- P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) for mutually exclusive events
- All probability rules derive from these
