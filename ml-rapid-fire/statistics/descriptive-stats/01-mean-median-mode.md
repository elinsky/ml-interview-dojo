# Mean, Median, Mode

**Q:** When should you use mean vs median vs mode?
- Definition of each
- Robustness to outliers
- When to prefer each

**A:**

- **Mean**: arithmetic average; sensitive to outliers
- **Median**: middle value; robust to outliers
- **Mode**: most frequent value; works for categorical data
- **When to use**:
  - Mean: symmetric distributions, need mathematical properties (e.g., for CLT)
  - Median: skewed data, outliers present (e.g., income, house prices, trade PnL)
  - Mode: categorical data, finding most common category
