# What is an Estimator?

**Q:** What is an estimator in statistics?
- Definition
- Estimator vs estimate
- Examples

**A:**

- **Definition**: a rule/function that maps sample data to a guess of a population parameter
- **Estimator vs estimate**: estimator is the formula (random variable); estimate is the computed value from specific data
- **Notation**: θ̂ (theta-hat) denotes an estimator of parameter θ
- **Examples**:
  - Sample mean x̄ estimates population mean μ
  - Sample variance s² estimates population variance σ²
  - Sample proportion p̂ estimates population proportion p
