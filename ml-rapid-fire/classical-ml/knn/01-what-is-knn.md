# What is KNN?

**Q:** What is K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)?

**A:**

- **Instance-based** (lazy) learning — stores entire training dataset as the model
- Also called: **case-based learning**, lazy learning, non-parametric
- Predicts based on the **K closest** training examples in feature space
- Used for both **classification** and **regression**
- Non-parametric — makes no assumptions about functional form
- Decision boundary can be arbitrarily complex
