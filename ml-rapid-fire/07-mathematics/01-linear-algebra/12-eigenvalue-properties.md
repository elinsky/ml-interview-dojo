# Eigenvalue Properties

**Q:** What are key properties of eigenvalues?
- Trace and determinant relationships
- Symmetric matrix eigenvalues
- Effect of matrix operations on eigenvalues

**A:**

- **Trace**: sum of eigenvalues = trace(A) = Σᵢ Aᵢᵢ
- **Determinant**: product of eigenvalues = det(A)
- **Symmetric matrix**: all eigenvalues are real; eigenvectors are orthogonal
- **Matrix operations**:
  - Eigenvalues of A⁻¹ are 1/λ
  - Eigenvalues of Aᵏ are λᵏ
  - Eigenvalues of A + cI are λ + c
- **Positive definite**: all eigenvalues > 0
