# Famous CNN Architectures

**Q:** What are the landmark CNN architectures?

**A:**

- **LeNet** (1998): first successful CNN (digit recognition)
- **AlexNet** (2012): won ImageNet, started deep learning boom
- **VGGNet** (2014): simple 3x3 filters, very deep
- **Inception/GoogLeNet** (2014): parallel filters of different sizes
- **ResNet** (2015): skip connections, 100+ layers
