# Dot Product

**Q:** What is the dot product and what does it measure?
- Formula
- Geometric interpretation
- Key properties
- ML applications

**A:**

- **Formula**: a · b = Σᵢ aᵢbᵢ = ‖a‖‖b‖cos(θ)
- **Geometric interpretation**: projection of one vector onto another; measures alignment
- **Properties**:
  - Commutative: a · b = b · a
  - Distributive: a · (b + c) = a · b + a · c
  - a · a = ‖a‖² (squared length)
- **ML applications**: similarity measures, attention scores, linear layer computations (Wx)
